A Brief History About Computer Force

The business was sold in 1983 and by 1999 another nine stores were opened. In July 2000, it was purchased by private equity bankers and senior management with the aim of taking the successful model nationally.


In October 2003, it was floated on the Australian Stock Exchange. Now, maintaining Barbuto's original philosophy, it is one of Australasia's fastest growing and largest retailer of home entertainment.


In July 2004, it bought the Queensland Clive Anthony chain of stores which sell consumer electronics, white goods, cooking appliances and air-conditioning. These locations have now been converted into HOME stores, where you can find everything you love about it more.


Now it offers the world's leading brands of Computers, Tablets, TVs, Cameras, Hi-Fi, Speakers, Home Theatre, Portable Audio, the largest range of games, recorded music, DVD music, Blu-Ray and DVD movies and TV shows and stacks more all at cheap prices! Everything you're after is available in one of our stores or online. We have all you need - best brands, huge range, cheapest prices, convenient locations, but most importantly genuine personal service from our experienced specialist staff.
